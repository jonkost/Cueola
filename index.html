What happens at this moment?
A cue is one instruction — something that happens at a specific moment in your show. Pick the type that best matches. Not sure? VIDEO is the most common starting point.
🎥VIDEO
Camera shots, live feeds, or clips to air
e.g. Take CAM 1 — anchor wide
🎙AUDIO
Mics, music, or sound cues
e.g. Anchor mics hot, fade music
📊GRAPHIC
Titles, lower thirds, fullscreen GFX
e.g. Show open title card
▶️PLAYBACK
Pre-recorded video or audio rolls
e.g. VT roll — news package
💡LIGHTING
Lighting presets or scene changes
e.g. Preset 4 — warm wash
📝SCRIPT
Anchor copy — syncs to prompter
e.g. Anchor reads cold open
🔀FLEXIBLE
Shape the journey — content TBD
e.g. Guest intro, set context ~3 min
⚓ANCHOR PROMPT
A redirect if conversation stalls
e.g. "What surprised you most about..."
⏸WIGGLE ROOM
Flex buffer — always build this in
Pros always leave wiggle room
🎬TAKE
Director take — switch to a source
e.g. Take CAM 1 — anchor wide
⚡READY
Standby — cue coming next
e.g. Ready CAM 2
📼VT ROLL
Roll pre-recorded video tape
e.g. VT roll — news package
📋LOWER 3RD
Lower third graphic overlay
e.g. Name/title lower third
🏃TOSS
Hand off to another presenter
e.g. Toss to field reporter
